Other Time Wasters

Some fun sites to check out (and get lost on)
Cookie Clicker Infamous. Click to make cookies. Spend cookies on stuff to make more cookies. So many cookies. Also, beware the grandmas.
Candy Box 2 Definitely less famous, but still fun and definitely a timesink. Collect candies and lollipops while adventuring, all in ASCII art!
2048 A sliding puzzle. Combine numbers together as much as possible and see how far you can go.
Find the Invisible Cow Listen to the sound. Try to find the cow (or goat, or other animal later) in this internet Hot and Cold.
HackerTyper Mash the keyboard to look like you're a TV hacker! Try hitting alt and caps a few times just for kicks.
The Oregon Trail The classic MSDos game, now right in your browser!
